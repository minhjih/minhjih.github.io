Short Bio

I am an M.S./Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, focusing on AI-driven wireless systems and digital twin simulations. My work explores robust positioning and learning-based control across simulated and real-world environments.

Research interests include AI-RAN, digital twin environments, ray-tracing simulation, foundation models, and robot control.
